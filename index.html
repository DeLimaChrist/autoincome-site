No. Results depend on the niche, offer, traffic, consistency, and market demand. We provide the structure and tools, not guaranteed earnings.

How do you get paid?

Usually through a setup fee and an agreed percentage of revenue. The exact terms can vary by offer and complexity.

Can this become a bigger company later?

Yes. This model can begin as a service business and later expand into a platform, agency, or software product.
